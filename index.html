⚗️ How to Play

📋 Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Look at the target temperature shown on the right.
  2. Tap HEAT ↑ to raise the temperature or COOL ↓ to lower it.
  3. Watch out! Temperature drifts back to room temperature (25°C) on its own.
  4. Reach and HOLD the target temperature for 2 seconds to win the round.
  5. Complete all 10 rounds without losing all 3 lives or running out of time.

💡 Game Tips

  • Temperature drifts! Tap continuously to fight it back.
  • Watch the thermometer — don't overshoot the target!
  • Some rounds require holding steady for 2–3 seconds.
  • Phase changes (boiling = 100°C, freezing = 0°C) are physics clues!
  • Later rounds add thermal inertia — buttons respond with delay!
